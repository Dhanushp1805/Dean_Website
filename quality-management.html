Quality Control Management

At Dean Textiles, we ensure that every order is meticulously monitored from sampling to shipment. We adopt a proactive approach in identifying potential issues and take immediate corrective actions to ensure timely deliveries. Our robust quality management system ensures the highest standards at each production stage.

Pre-Production Check (PPC): We minimize risks by thoroughly checking materials, components, and accessories before production starts, ensuring everything meets the required standards.

Initial Production Check (IPC): The first finished products are checked against the buyer’s specifications and prototype sample. Any deviations are immediately identified and corrected.

During Production Check (DUPRO): We inspect products during production to ensure that earlier discrepancies are resolved and that production runs maintain a consistent quality standard.

Final Random Inspection (FIR): A final random inspection is conducted when the consignment is ready for shipment, based on international inspection standards. We examine samples randomly for design, shrinkage, accessories, appearance, and more.
