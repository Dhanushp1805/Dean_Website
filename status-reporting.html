Status Reporting Service

Status report based on the multi-state inspections are transmitted to the buyers by fax or e.mail. Production progress is analyzed in relation to the delivery terms and suitable course of action s taken to ensure goods are delivered in time. The consignment is allowed for shipment if only it is upto the buyers required standard.
At Dean Textiles, we provide comprehensive status reporting solutions to keep you informed at every stage of the production process.

Regular Updates: We send regular updates on sampling, production, and shipment status directly to the buyers via fax or email, ensuring that you are always informed about the progress of your orders.

Proactive Actions: Our team analyzes the production progress in relation to delivery terms and takes necessary actions to ensure timely deliveries, while addressing any potential issues immediately.
