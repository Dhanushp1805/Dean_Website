Ensuring Quality Through Comprehensive Laboratory Testing Service

At Dean Textiles, our laboratory testing services are designed to minimize quality risks and uphold international standards. We provide thorough testing solutions for fabrics, colors, garments, and more, ensuring reliability at every stage.

  • Fabric strength.
  • Dimensional stability.
  • Color fastness.
  • Seam analysis.
  • Garment tests.
  • Quality checks.

Comprehensive Laboratory Testing Services

Laboratories are an integral part of our services, ensuring manufacturers and exporters can conduct their business with minimal quality risk.

Fabric Testing: Construction particulars (counts, constructions, weight), strength, measurement, and seam performance are meticulously analyzed.

Analysis: Tests include flammability, dimensional stability (shrinkage) to washing/dry cleaning, appearance after washing/dry cleaning, and fiber analysis.

Color Fastness: Evaluations for color fastness to washing, perspiration, light, rubbing, bleaching, dry cleaning, organic solvents, and hot pressing. Dyes and pigment tests are also conducted.

Garment Testing: Dimensional stability and appearance after washing or dry cleaning are thoroughly tested to meet quality requirements.
